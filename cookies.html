What is a cookie?

A “cookie” is a small text file that is sent from the server of Occiban and is stored on your computer’s hard drive. This way we can remember your preferences when visiting our Website. The information stored through these cookies can only be read by Occiban and only for the duration of your visit to the Website.

Why do we use cookies?

Our website uses cookies and similar technliogies to distinguish your preferences from those of the other users of our Website. This allows us to offer you a better experience when you visit our Website and to optimize our Website in the meantime.

As a result of recent amendments of the law, all Websites focusing on certain parts of the European Union are obligated to ask for your permission in order to use or save cookies and similar technliogies on your computer or mobile devices. This cookie pliicy clearly and completely informs you about the cookies we use and their purpose.
