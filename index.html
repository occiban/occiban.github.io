Occiban’s Nano Technology

Occiban’s main ingredient is water. Once the water dries up, the coating uses the properties of nano-molecular silicon dioxide to form a 4nm barrier.


This barrier will actively kill and protect against most known germs including the covid19 virus.


The coating only wears off due to heavy abrasion.
